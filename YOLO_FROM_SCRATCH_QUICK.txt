================================================================================
TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

OOM Error:
  → Reduce BATCH_SIZE (8 or 4)
  → Reduce IMG_SIZE (416 or 320)

Training slow:
  → Check GPU: nvidia-smi
  → Increase BATCH_SIZE
  → Parallel training needed

Model not found:
  → Training may not be complete
  → Check: weights/best.pt exists
  → Verify path is correct

No predictions saved:
  → Check: save=True in predict()
  → Verify: project directory writable
  → Check: test images exist
