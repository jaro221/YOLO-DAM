This script provides a COMPLETE pipeline for training and testing YOLO models
from scratch:

├─ YOLOv8: 3 models (nano, medium, xlarge)

├─ Save annotated prediction images
├─ Save YOLO format predictions with confidence
└─ Organize results by version
   Time: ~30-80 minutes total
   Output: ~16,000-32,000 prediction files
